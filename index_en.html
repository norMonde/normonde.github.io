What am I?

Hello, I'm Ronan, and above all, I'm a passionate traveler and sea enthusiast! I've always dreamed of taking a grand journey after finishing my studies. Earning my engineering degree in 2021 seemed like the perfect time to embark on this adventure... but then COVID-19 hit, closing borders worldwide. So, I decided to postpone my trip until 2024 and pursued a PhD in applied mathematics instead. Now, I've completed my doctorate, specializing in beach sediment dynamics. All that's left is to prepare for my journey and set off! Fingers crossed that monkeypox doesn't spark another global pandemic...

In my youth, I was fortunate to have parents who loved to travel! Many of our trips were meaningful to me, helping shape the person I am today. However, the experience that had the greatest impact on me was living in Japan from the age of 3 to 6. Even at such a young age, I was deeply moved and fascinated by the cultural differences. Japan will always hold a special place in my heart, and I hope to return someday!

The past few years have been instrumental in shaping my plans. Spending days in an office can sometimes fuel a strong desire to break free. While my eight years as a beach lifeguard allowed me to experience a hands-on, outdoor profession, each rescue infused with a sense of adventure, they also sparked a deeper longing for exploration. My weekend outings, cross-country journeys, and hitchhiking trips across France have further ignited my passion for discovering the world through hitchhiking. I've come to believe that travel shouldn't be limited to a three-week round-trip flight, only to return to the daily grind. Instead, it should be about embracing a more profound and continuous journey of discovery.

For me, this journey is an opportunity to become an ambassador for hitchhiking and alternative travel methods, especially in the context of the ongoing ecological crisis.
