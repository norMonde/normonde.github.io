About Me

What am I?

Hello, I'm Ronan, and I'm above all a lover of the sea and travelling! I've always thought about doing a big trip after my studies. My engineering degree in 2021 was the perfect transition to travel... But that wasn't counting the COVID-19, which closed all borders at that time. So I decided to postpone my trip until 2024 by doing a PhD in applied mathematics. Now I have a doctorate in applied mathematics on beach sediment dynamics. All I have to do now is prepare for my trip and leave! Here's hoping that monkeypox doesn't create a new global pandemic...

In my youth, I was lucky enough to have parents who loved to travel! Many of our journeys were significant for me, they served to build the individual that I am. But the experience that had the greatest impact on me was when I went to live in Japan from the age of 3 to 6. Despite my young age, I was still overwhelmed and amazed by this culture shock. Japan will remain in my heart forever and I hope to be able to go back one day!

The last few years have helped me to build my project. Indeed, spending days in an office can sometimes instil a desire to escape.Although my 8 years as a beach lifeguard gave me the opportunity to discover a human and above all outdoor profession, with a touch of adventure in each of our interventions, they also awakened in me a desire to go further in the adventure.My weekends out, my crossings of France and my hitchhiking trips have helped me to awaken this desire to discover the world by hitchhiking.It has become essential to show that travelling is not just about taking a return plane ticket over 3 weeks and going back to the routine of work.

For me, this trip is a way of becoming an ambassador for hitchhiking and new travel, in this context of ecological crisis.

My Project

Hitchhiking around the world!

Description of my Itinerary

There are two possible routes on this itinerary. The first possibility is to reach America from France at the start of the adventure. This would make it ‘easy’ to find the first ocean crossing, while remaining in a French-speaking environment. The second possibility would be to set off directly for Asia, via the Caspian Sea to avoid the political situations in Iran and Russia. This would allow us to get straight into the swing of auto-strop, but it would still be necessary to make two crossings by hitchhiking boat.

So the ideal is to manage to set off by boat-hitchhiking directly from France to reach Latin America. Then visit the continent by hitchhiking.
